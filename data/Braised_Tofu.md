Introduce:
Quail eggs are really good for health, especially for kids. Today, Vietnamtourism will introduce Braised Tofu with Quail Eggs Recipe (Trứng Cút Kho Đậu Hũ) which comes from many amazing Vietnamese Recipes. Keeping many good benefits and easy for cooking, your kids and all members in family will fall in love with it.
Ingredients:
100 – 200g fried tofu slices
15 – 20 quail eggs
Spring onion, shallot, salt, fish sauce, sugar, pepper, soy sauce and Maggi’s stuff (Maggi's seasoning powder).
Process:
Step 1: Slice fried tofu into square shapes.
Step 2: Boil quail eggs, peel off the cover. Clean and slice small spring onion.
Step 3: Heat 2 teaspoons oil, fry sliced shallot until it is fragrant. Next, add quail eggs, tofu, 2 tablespoons fish sauce, ½ teaspoon Maggi’s stuff, 1 teaspoon sugar, ½ teaspoon salt, 1 teaspoon soy sauce, shake gently to make sure all stuffs are absorbed these spices.
Step 4: Next, cook with large heat in 2 – 3 minutes. Add a little water and continue cooking with small heat 20 – 30 minutes. Season again to suit your flavour, sprinkle more sliced spring onion + pepper on top and turn off the heat.
Finally: I suggest you should eat with steamed rice and hot soup.
